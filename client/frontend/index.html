How Food Analyser Works - 3 easy steps

Analyse your food quickly and make healthier choices in just three simple steps.

  • 01

    Upload or Scan Food

    Upload a clear image of your food or scan the nutrition label to begin analysing ingredients, nutritional values, and overall health impact. Our smart food analyser quickly processes the information and provides easy-to-understand insights to help you make healthier, more informed dietary choices.

  • 02

    Ingredient Analysis

    Our system carefully examines each ingredient to identify potential allergens, harmful additives, artificial preservatives, and other concerning components. It then provides a clear, easy-to-understand health evaluation of the product, helping you quickly assess whether it aligns with your dietary needs and wellness goals.

  • 03

    Get Suggestions & Track History

    Receive personalized tips, healthier alternatives, and smart recommendations based on your food analysis results. Easily track your previous scans and analyses to monitor patterns, improve your eating habits, and stay consistent with your long-term health and nutrition goals.
